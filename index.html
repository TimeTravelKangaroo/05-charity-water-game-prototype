Flow to Me

A puzzle game for

Flow to Me is a puzzle game about bringing clean water to every village. It's inspired by the work of charity: water, a nonprofit that helps communities around the world get access to clean, safe drinking water.
How to play:
  • Use tiles to create water paths from wells to all villages
  • Drag and drop to swap tiles and tap to rotate them
